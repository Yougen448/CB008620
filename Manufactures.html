Green Plastic

Green Plastic Industries mission is to manufacture Environmentally friendly household items that are used for our daily life. Moreover, the products by Green Plastic Industry are compostable and biodegradable in both water and carbon dioxide. They also manufacture products such as eco-friendly cups,eco-friendly plates and eco-friendly bags.

Harleys PLC began its business sales as Chas P. Hayley & Company in 1878. So, from 1878 till now the company is well known for its coir based fabric products globally.
